CURRENT SUPREME COURT OF NIGERIA JUSTICES (2024)
================================================

CHIEF JUSTICE OF NIGERIA
========================

Justice Kudirat Motonmori Olatokunbo Kekere-Ekun, GCON
------------------------------------------------------
Appointed: August 22, 2024
Born: May 7, 1958
State of Origin: Ogun State
Education:
- University of Lagos (LL.B, 1980)
- Nigerian Law School (BL, 1981)
- Called to Nigerian Bar: 1981

Career Progression:
- Private Practice (1981-1989)
- High Court Judge, Lagos State (1989-2004)
- Court of Appeal Justice (2004-2013)
- Supreme Court Justice (2013-2024)
- Chief Justice of Nigeria (2024-present)

Notable Achievements:
- First female Chief Justice from Southwest Nigeria
- Specialist in commercial and constitutional law
- Advocate for judicial reforms and gender equality
- Led several landmark constitutional cases

Areas of Expertise:
- Constitutional Law
- Commercial Law
- Human Rights
- Judicial Administration
